# ExamPull Blockers

Last updated: 2026-05-01

No human blockers are currently accepted. The blank repository, missing generated env values, and unprovisioned services are build tasks in scope, not blockers.

## Blocker Format

When a true blocker appears, record:

- What was attempted
- What failed
- Exact decision or credential needed
- Options available
- Recommended path

Vague entries are not blockers and must be converted into executable work.
